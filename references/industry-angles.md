# Industry-Specific Copy Angles

> Vertical-specific pain points, objections, SMS hooks, email angles, discovery questions, and FAQ topics.
> Use the matching vertical when generating client copy. If no match, use Universal.

---

## 1. MEDICAL / WELLNESS
*Med spa, dental, chiro, PT, weight loss, aesthetics*

**Pain Points:** No-shows killing revenue, leads going cold before front desk calls back, competing on price with groupon/discount shops, can't track which ads work, staff too busy to follow up
**Top Objections:** "Is it covered by insurance?", "How many treatments?", "I need to check my schedule", "I've had a bad experience before", "Too expensive"
**SMS Hooks:** "Still dealing with [symptom]?", "Your spot is filling up fast for [treatment]", "Quick question about the [treatment] you asked about"
**Email Angles:** Before/after transformation stories, "what most people don't know about [treatment]", seasonal offers tied to events
**Discovery Questions:** Monthly patient volume? Average ticket size? Show rate? How fast does front desk call back new leads? Top 3 services by revenue?
**FAQ Topics:** Insurance acceptance, treatment duration, recovery time, qualifications, before/after results, booking process

## 2. HOME SERVICES
*HVAC, solar, roofing, plumbing, pest control, landscaping*

**Pain Points:** Losing jobs to whoever calls back first, seasonal demand swings, leads from HomeAdvisor/Thumbtack are garbage, can't afford full-time appointment setters, truck rolls on unqualified leads
**Top Objections:** "Just get me an estimate", "I'm getting other quotes", "Not sure I need it right now", "How much does it cost?", "Can you come out this week?"
**SMS Hooks:** "Hey [name], you requested a quote for [service]. Still need that taken care of?", "Most [city] homeowners overpay for [service] by $[X]. Want to see why?", "Quick one. Still dealing with [problem]?"
**Email Angles:** "The 37-minute rule" (speed to lead stats), seasonal maintenance tips, "what your neighbor paid for [service]", before/after project showcase
**Discovery Questions:** Service area radius? Average job size? Lead sources? Current response time? Close rate on estimates? Peak season?
**FAQ Topics:** Pricing/estimates, timeline, warranty, licensing, service area, emergency availability

## 3. REAL ESTATE
*Agents, investors, property management*

**Pain Points:** Leads go cold in hours not days, Zillow/Realtor leads are expensive and shared, can't follow up consistently while showing houses, database of past clients sitting unused, no system for sphere of influence
**Top Objections:** "I already have an agent", "Just browsing", "Market is too [hot/cold]", "What's your commission?", "How many homes have you sold?"
**SMS Hooks:** "Hey [name], saw you were looking at homes in [area]. Still searching?", "Quick update on [area] market. Prices just [moved]. Worth knowing.", "[name], new listing just hit in [area]. Want first look?"
**Email Angles:** Monthly market updates, "what sold in your neighborhood", home value estimator, "3 mistakes first-time buyers make", client success story
**Discovery Questions:** Average deal size? Leads per month? Lead sources? Follow-up process? Close rate? Days on market?
**FAQ Topics:** Commission, process timeline, pre-approval, market conditions, comparable sales, staging

## 4. INSURANCE
*Health, auto, life, commercial*

**Pain Points:** Quoting takes too long and they shop around, compliance requirements slow everything down, retention/renewals falling off, leads from aggregators are low quality, can't differentiate from competitors
**Top Objections:** "I need to compare quotes", "My current agent handles everything", "I don't want to switch", "Is this a sales call?", "Just email me the info"
**SMS Hooks:** "Hey [name], found a way to save you about $[X]/month on [insurance type]. Got 2 min?", "Quick question. When's the last time someone actually reviewed your [policy type]?", "[name], rates just changed for [state]. Could affect your premium."
**Email Angles:** "The policy gap nobody checks", rate comparison stories, "what happens if you're underinsured", annual review reminders
**Discovery Questions:** Lines of business? Average policy value? Lead sources? Quote-to-bind ratio? Retention rate? Compliance requirements?
**FAQ Topics:** Coverage types, claims process, rate factors, switching process, bundling discounts, cancellation policy

## 5. FUNDING / LENDING
*Business funding, SaaS lending, mortgage*

**Pain Points:** Applicants damage their credit by applying everywhere (shotgun approach), funding companies promise no upfront fee but deliver garbage, long approval timelines kill urgency, clients don't follow through on applications
**Top Objections:** "I can do this myself", "What's the catch?", "Will this hurt my credit?", "I've been denied before", "How much can I get approved for?"
**SMS Hooks:** "Hey [name], quick question about the funding you applied for. Still need that capital?", "Most business owners leave $[X]k on the table because they apply wrong. 2 min to explain?", "[name], your profile looks like you could qualify for $[X]k+. Worth a call?"
**Email Angles:** "The biggest mistake in business funding", "why banks say no (and how to fix it)", case study with specific approval amounts, "one shot to get this right"
**Discovery Questions:** Credit score range? Existing business revenue? Time in business? What they need funding for? Previous applications? Target funding amount?
**FAQ Topics:** Credit impact, approval timeline, required documents, funding amounts, repayment terms, qualification requirements

## 6. COACHING / INFO PRODUCTS
*Course creators, coaches, consultants*

**Pain Points:** Selling a "me too" course in a saturated market, low webinar show rates, high refund rates, can't articulate their unique mechanism, spending on ads with no follow-up system
**Top Objections:** "I've bought courses before and didn't finish", "How is this different?", "I don't have time", "Can I see results from other students?", "That's expensive for a course"
**SMS Hooks:** "Hey [name], you signed up for [event/lead magnet]. What made you interested?", "Quick question. Are you still trying to [desired result]?", "Most [their ICP] stay stuck because they [biggest mistake]. Sound familiar?"
**Email Angles:** Student transformation stories, "the real reason your [business/skill] isn't growing", behind-the-scenes of the program, "what I'd do if I was starting over"
**Discovery Questions:** What do they teach? Average customer value? Current student count? Lead sources? Show rate on webinars? Biggest objection on sales calls?
**FAQ Topics:** Program duration, time commitment, access/format, results timeline, guarantee, support level

## 7. SAAS / B2B
*Software companies, agencies, professional services*

**Pain Points:** Long sales cycles, multiple decision makers, leads need education before buying, free trials that don't convert, churn after 3 months
**Top Objections:** "We need to involve the team", "How does it integrate with [tool]?", "What's the implementation timeline?", "We're locked into a contract", "Can we do a pilot?"
**SMS Hooks:** "Hey [name], saw your team is looking at [solution category]. Quick question before your demo.", "Most [role] we talk to are dealing with [specific pain]. That ring true for you?", "[name], got something that might save your team [X hours/week]. 2 min?"
**Email Angles:** ROI calculators, "how [similar company] saved $X/month", implementation timeline breakdowns, "the hidden cost of [their current tool]"
**Discovery Questions:** Team size? Current tools? Monthly spend on [category]? Biggest bottleneck? Decision makers? Implementation concerns? Contract status?
**FAQ Topics:** Integration, security/compliance, implementation timeline, pricing/seats, support, data migration

## 8. AUTOMOTIVE
*Dealers, detailing, repair*

**Pain Points:** Online leads never answer the phone, price shoppers waste time, service department follow-up is nonexistent, can't track which leads turned into sales, seasonal slowdowns
**Top Objections:** "Just looking", "What's my trade-in worth?", "Can you beat this price?", "I need to think about it", "My credit isn't great"
**SMS Hooks:** "Hey [name], the [vehicle/service] you asked about is still available. Want to come check it out?", "Quick one. Still in the market for [vehicle type]?", "[name], we've got a special on [service] this week. Interested?"
**Email Angles:** "Why the price you see online isn't the real price", new inventory alerts, service maintenance tips, trade-in value updates
**Discovery Questions:** Monthly lead volume? Lead sources? Average deal size? Close rate on internet leads? Follow-up process? BDC team size?
**FAQ Topics:** Financing options, trade-in process, warranty, service scheduling, inventory availability, delivery timeline
